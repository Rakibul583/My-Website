Project 3

"Air Quality Prediction: A Comparative Study of ARIMA and SARIMA Models" explores forecasting air quality using time series models. The project involves analyzing historical pollution data, identifying trends and seasonality, and implementing ARIMA and SARIMA models. Their performance is evaluated using metrics like MAE and RMSE to determine their effectiveness. The study provides insights into the strengths and limitations of both models, offering guidance for selecting the best approach for air quality forecasting, with potential applications in environmental management and policy-making.
